Hi, I'm Cooper Hollmaier. I'm a people-first leader with a passion for developing digital products that are fast, sustainable, and accessible for all.

Currently, I work with a talented team to support the technical search engine optimization (SEO) of REI's (Recreational Equipment, Inc.) digital properties. Prior to coming in-house, I worked agency-side with brands including BarkShop, Gerber Childrenswear, Plant Therapy, and A.T. Cross.

When I'm not in the office, you'll find me embracing the outdoors. I'm an avid mountain biker, runner, and am always up for the next adventure.

Want to connect? Please don't hesitate to reach out on Twitter or email me.
