Cooper Hollmaier

Associate Program Manager, Technical SEO

Cooper Hollmaier is an Associate Program Manager at REI (Recreational Equipment, Inc.) in Seattle, Washington, where he works with a talented team to support the technical search engine optimization (SEO) of REI's digital properties. Before coming in-house, Cooper worked agency-side with brands including BarkShop, Gerber Childrenswear, Plant Therapy, and A.T. Cross.

Cooper is an avid mountain biker, runner, and outdoors-man. Never turning down the next adventure, you can find him and his wife, Janessa scoping out the next dog-friendly trail or restaurant to check off their bucket-list.
